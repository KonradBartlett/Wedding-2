Transportation

Getting to Peterborough

There are a number of ways to get to Peterborough, but the most common are:

  • Driving
  • GO Transit
    - If you want to take GO Transit you'll need to take the train to Oshawa and then take the bus to Peterborough. The bus will drop you off at the downtown bus terminal which is walking distance to the Holiday Inn

Getting to the Venue

There will be a shuttle bus running from downtown Peterborough to the venue. Pickup for the shuttle bus to the event will be at 12:30pm, and 1:00pm, and pickup for the busses leaving the venue will be 10:00pm, 11:00pm, and 11:30pm.

The shuttle bus will be picking up and dropping off at the parking lot for the best western.

The bus carries 30 people at a time. There will be 2 trips to the event, and 3 trips from the event.

Peterborough does have Uber, Lyft, and Taxis, however the chances of them driving out to Keene at midnight on a Saturday are very unlikely.

Parking at the venue

There is parking available at the venue, however we strongly encourage you to take the shuttle bus if you can.

If you're driving to the event be sure to have a designated driver and to drive safe!

Our Engagement Story

The Decision

While attending Maggie's sister's wedding on December 17th, 2022. Seeing the celebration of everyone there, and the bond that Amy and Brennan shared, Konrad thought 'I wanna do this'.

Konrad knew the moment that this photo was taken that he was going to propse.

The Adventure

Konrad saved up all his money and bought the ring. When they took their annual bike trip to Victoria BC, in June, Konrad knew he was going to propose. Eager for what awaited them in the future they cycled on.

But Maggie was completely unaware that this trip would change her life forever.

The Escape

They found themselves trapped in a dark, haunted, escape room. The only way out was to solve puzzles, but when they got to the final room they found a locked chest. Opening the chest revealed a small velvet box. Konrad quickly grabbed got down on one knee and asked Maggie to marry him.

Maggie said yes, and they escaped the room.

The Celebration

To celebrate the two went to dine at the finest restaurant in all of Victoria, Aura Waterfront. They had a wonderful meal, ordering everything on the menu and discovering their love of oysters. Afterwards they went outside to enjoy the sunset and took a water taxi across the bay back to their stay.

They spent the rest of the trip enjoying the city, and each other's company.

And Now They're Getting Married!
