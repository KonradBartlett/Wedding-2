There are several ways to get to Peterborough, but the most practical are:

  • Driving
  • GO Transit

Our Engagement Story

The Decision

In 2022, Maggie & Konrad attended Maggie's sisters wedding. The union of Amy and Brennan was beautiful and Konrad, seeing the bond they both shared, though: "I want to do this."

Konrad knew the moment that this photo was taken that he was going to propose soon.

The Adventure

Konrad saved up his money and bought the ring. When Maggie and Konrad took their annual bike trip to Victoria BC, in June, Konrad secretly knew he was going to propose. Eager for what awaited them in the future they cycled on.

But Maggie was completely unaware that this trip would change her life forever.

The Escape

They found themselves trapped in a dark, haunted, escape room. The only way out was to solve puzzles, but when they got to the final room they found a locked chest. Upon opening the chest a small velvet box revealed itself. Konrad snatched the box quickly then got down on one knee and asked Maggie to marry him.

Maggie said YES!!, and they escaped the room.

The Celebration

To celebrate their engagement, the couple went to dine at the finest restaurant in all of Victoria, Aura Waterfront. They had a spectacular meal, (which included - ordering everything on the menu and discovering their shared love of oysters.) Afterwards, they went outside to enjoy the sunset and to take a water taxi across the bay.

They spent the rest of their trip sightseeing, but most importantly, enjoying each other's company.

And Now They're Getting Married!
